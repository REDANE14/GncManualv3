  • DISSAMBLE AND ASSEMBLE OF COMPUTER

Instruction

Tips! Prepare a screwdriver and saucer plate for all screws. Make sure that the pc is unplugged to a live source outlet. Handle each parts with care. Goodluck!

  1. Remove all side panel of the computer case.
  2. Lay down the hollow side of the case for easy access to other parts.
  3. Remove all cables attached to the motherboard. Press down the latch before pulling the cable.
  4. Remove the RAM, by releasing the locking clips and proceed to pull out the RAM.
  5. Remove the graphics card, by releasing the locking clips and proceed to pull out graphics card with care. Place the card to any non-conductive surface.
  6. Remove the heatsink and its power cable. This will expose the CPU
  7. Lift the lever and remove the CPU from the socket.
  8. Grab a screwdriver, locate all the screws and proceed to unscrew then place to the saucer plate.
  9. Gently remove the motherboard in the case.
  10. Remove the power supply to the pc case. Great work!
